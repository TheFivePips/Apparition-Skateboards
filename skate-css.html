Apparition Skateboards is a local Austin, TX skate shop that has been in operation since September of 2008. Originally opened as The Lone Star Sk8, then re-founded in 2009 as Apparition. Apparition is about customer service and building the skating community in Austin, TX. We are proud to offer a vast selection of quality products. Apparition is a hard-goods concentric shop with a great selection of the best new and old school.

Build a custom complete with the help of our knowledgeable staff!

From longboards to penny boards, we have all the most popular brands of skate parts.

Shirts, shoes and more amazing apparel to match your skating needs and style!
